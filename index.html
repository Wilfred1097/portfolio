About Me

I am a passionate Programmer and Web Developer with expertise in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, PHP, MySQL, Python, Robot Programming, and IoT. Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Western Mindanao State University, I specialize in developing innovative web applications and IoT-based solutions that integrate hardware and software for automation and intelligent systems.
During my time as a Programmer/Web Developer at Malindang Broadcasting Network, I developed an Insurance System to manage payment histories and automate reporting, as well as a Product Management System for tracking inventory and sales. Additionally, I played a key role as the lead programmer in our thesis, focusing on system development, code optimization, and hardware-software integration.
With a strong background in embedded systems and IoT, I have successfully implemented Arduino and microcontroller-based projects that enhance automation and efficiency. I am always eager to learn new technologies, solve complex problems, and contribute to innovative software solutions.
